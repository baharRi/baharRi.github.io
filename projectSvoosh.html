 Spring 2022

Interaction design for android application

In the class of IN2000: Software Engineering (Spring 2022), I worked in an interdisciplinary team with other informatics students to create an application made to guide the people of Oslo on their bicycling journeys. The app uses APIs from the Norwegian Meteorological Institute and was created through agile software development.

Within the team I was given the role as head designer, and my responsibilities were to plan interactions with users as well as creating design mockups and implementing the design into the app using the markup language XML. The rest of the application was created using Kotlin, which I also contributed to occasionally.
