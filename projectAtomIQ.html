Interaction layer for crypto investment application

In the class of IN3010: Transformative Design (Spring 2023), with a focus on social sustainability, my design team partnered with the established team from AtomIQ to create a wireframe of the interaction design layer of their upcoming investment app.

This mockup was not focused on aesthetics, but rather how we could contribute our expertise within HCI and creativity as students to create an application that encourages and educates new users for the sake of democracy within the field of finance.
