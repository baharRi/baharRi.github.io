About me

I am eager to collaborate with teams of fellow designers as well as other disciplines in order to craft technology that serves as a tool for empowerment and helpfulness, rather than contributing to exclusion.

My projects



AtomIQ

Interaction design for crypto investment app

In the class of IN3010: Transformative Design (Spring 2023), with a focus on social sustainability, my design team partnered with an established team of developers to create a wireframe of the interaction design layer of their upcoming investment app AtomIQ.

This mockup was not focused on aesthetics, but rather how we could contribute our expertise within HCI and creativity as students to create an application that encourages and educates new users for the sake of democracy within the field of finance.



Svoosh

Interaction design for android app

My responsibilities within the team was to create design mockups as well as implementing the design into the app using the markup language XML. The rest of the application was created using Kotlin, which I also contributed to occasionally.

P4L

Designing, building and Arduino-programming of

P4L the robot

In the class of IN1060: Use Oriented Design (Spring 2021), in a group with fellow design students, we created P4L: a friendly robot designed to encourage socializing and a sense of responsibility as well as fighting loneliness in a time of crisis and quarantine.


P4L was created using Arduino in order to implement functionality such as the ability to feed and pet the robot, as well as having it communicate through sounds and movements.


Project on GitHub: https://github.com/ltuhn/anemone
